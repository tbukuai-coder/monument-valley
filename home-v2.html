The trick — in your hands

Turn the bridge yourself

This is the game's real engine, not a video. There are no edge lists: two tiles connect whenever they look adjacent on screen — even two storeys apart in the grid. Drag the rose bridge until the road lines up, and the traveler will trust it with her life.

The journey

Ten skies, one road

Scroll, and the light will pass through every chapter — a wanderer's dawn, a mother and child, then open water and the last fragment of light. Each chapter has its own sky and teaches one idea. Progress saves in your browser.

Built like a monument

One file, proven solid

Chapters10
Levels provenall

The whole game — renderer, puzzles, sound — is one static HTML file with no build step. Every chapter is verified headlessly by a validator that replays the adjacency rule and searches the full joint state space (characters × rotation × ferry × tide × orb), proving each level is solvable, can't be solved without its mechanisms, hides no accidental shortcuts, and can never strand you. The soundtrack of steps, thocks, and chimes is a tiny WebAudio synth.
